ABOUT ME

I am Lamiya Safarova, I started my academic life at the Azerbaijan State University of Languages. During this period, I worked as an English teacher and facilitator according to my specialty. Later, I continued my studies in China. During the 3 years I lived there, I completed a one-year master's degree, worked as a teacher at a language center for 2 years, and as a teacher at an international school for 1 year. After returning from China, I continued my career by taking online English classes for a while.

My activity at Code Academy was more long-term and versatile. At Code Academy, I initially worked as an English teacher, then as a curriculum leader for English language teaching, and created digital literacy teaching in English. Then, as an education coordinator, I was involved in the quality of education, the recruitment of teachers, providing them with appropriate training and creating a curriculum. My next destination was Estonia, where I worked as an English teacher as well as an instructional designer in an American company.
